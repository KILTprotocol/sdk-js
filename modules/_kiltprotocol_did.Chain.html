getAddEndpointExtrinsic

  • getAddEndpointExtrinsic(endpoint: DidServiceEndpoint): Promise<Extrinsic>
    • Defined in did/src/Did.chain.ts:697

    Generate an extrinsic to add the provided DidServiceEndpoint to the authorising DID.

    Parameters

    • endpoint: DidServiceEndpoint

      The new service endpoint to include in the extrinsic. The service endpoint must respect the following conditions: - The service endpoint ID is at most 50 ASCII characters long and is a valid URI fragment according to RFC#3986. - The service endpoint has at most 1 service type, with a value that is at most 50 ASCII characters long. - The service endpoint has at most 1 URL, with a value that is at most 200 ASCII characters long, and which is a valid URI according to RFC#3986.

    Returns Promise<Extrinsic>

    An extrinsic that must be authorized (signed) by the FullDid with which the service endpoint should be associated.
